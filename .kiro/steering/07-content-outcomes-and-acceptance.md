# Content outcomes and acceptance criteria

## Content outcomes

The user must be able to:
- understand what this service does
- decide whether this service is relevant to them
- understand what they need before they start
- complete the transaction without unnecessary support
- recover from errors
- understand what happens after submission

## Content acceptance criteria

- Page headings explain the purpose of each page.
- Primary actions are clear and specific.
- Form labels are plain language.
- Hint text explains only what is needed.
- Error messages say what went wrong and how to fix it.
- Links describe their destination or action.
- Risky content is marked for owner confirmation.
- Customer-facing content targets Flesch Reading Ease 80+ and Grade Level below 10.
