# Content model templates

Use these templates when documenting content in design specs.

## Content model

For each page, define:

| Page | Purpose | Heading | Primary CTA | Secondary action | Key content | Content risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Microcopy inventory

| Component | Copy required | Draft copy | Source needed | Review status |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Error and validation content

| Field or action | Error condition | Error message | Recovery instruction |
|---|---|---|---|

## Readability target

Customer-facing content should target:
- Flesch Reading Ease 80+
- Flesch-Kincaid Grade below 10
- 0 spelling issues
- 0 grammar issues
