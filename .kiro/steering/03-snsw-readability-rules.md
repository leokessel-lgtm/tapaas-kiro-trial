# SNSW readability rules

Use these rules when reviewing content.

## Customer-facing content targets

- Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level: below 10
- Flesch Reading Ease: 80+
- Spelling issues: 0
- Grammar issues: 0
- Very long sentences: less than 6%
- Adverbs: less than 4%
- Clichés: less than 1%
- Grade: A
- Reach: 80%

## Technical or internal content targets

For technical or internal content, Flesch Reading Ease may be 60+ where the subject matter requires it.

Technical content still needs:
- abbreviations spelled out on first use
- complex terms explained
- one subject per paragraph
- reduced jargon
- supporting summaries where needed

## Human judgement

Readability formulas do not test whether content is accurate, helpful, policy-safe or using the right customer terms.

Always combine readability scores with human review.

## Boundary

Do not claim that content is WCAG compliant, legally approved, policy approved or publication ready.

Use safer wording:
- "The content appears to meet the readability target."
- "The content does not meet the readability target."
- "This needs content review."
- "This needs accessibility review."
- "This needs policy, legal or SME confirmation."
